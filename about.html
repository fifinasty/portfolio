About

It all started with a sketchbook, a curious eye, and an obsession with how things look and feel. That curiosity led me to graphic design - where I now specialize in brand identity, UX/UI design, and web development. I love building visual systems that make ideas clear, experiences smooth, and brands unforgettable.

Beyond design, I'm a multidisciplinary artist at heart. I've worked as a tattooist, an illustrator, and occasionally swap my stylus for a camera. When I'm not creating, you'll probably find me reading, sketching, or hunting for strange fonts and weirder inspiration. Basically, if it involves visuals and a bit of storytelling, I'm all in.
